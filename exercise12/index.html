Walnut Woods State Park

West Des Moines, IA

Return to Homepage

In this map of Walnut Woods State Park, I used Mapbox to first select a style. I used the outdoors template.
I then edited the style to make walking paths brown with dashed lines. I added a line shapefile that I collected while
hiking on the trial at Walnut Woods. I also added points of signs and info boards,(white pentagons with Xs). I used
ArcGIS Online to export these files as GeoJson files. After uploading these to my mapbox account, I changed the
style to better fit the map.
